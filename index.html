Welcome

The physical growth of tribal people is influenced by lifestyle, nutrition, environment, and cultural practices. This site explores the major factors.

Factors Affecting Physical Growth

  • Genetic background
  • Nutrition and diet patterns
  • Daily physical activities
  • Environmental conditions
  • Healthcare availability

Nutrition in Tribal Communities

Common foods found in tribal diets:

  • Millets and coarse grains
  • Forest vegetables and edible roots
  • Local fruits and berries
  • Meat, fish, and natural protein sources
  • Traditional herbal drinks

Challenges

  • Poor access to healthcare
  • Malnutrition in some communities
  • Lack of sanitation and clean water
  • Poverty and isolation
  • High physical labor from a young age
