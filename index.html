Unpacking the Challenges of Health and Nutrition

This website serves as an informational resource exploring the critical topic of physical growth patterns among tribal student populations, primarily in India.

Our Mission

To highlight the disparities in anthropometric indices (height, weight, BMI) and investigate the complex interplay of socio-economic, environmental, and cultural factors that affect the well-being and growth trajectory of these communities.

Did You Know?

Studies often show a higher prevalence of *stunting* and *underweight* among tribal students compared to non-tribal peers, indicating a profound impact of chronic nutritional and health challenges.
